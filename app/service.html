What does
cooperation look like?

We listen to all your questions

We will consult on the registration of a company, forms of ownership, tax systems. We select the best option for you. We sign the necessary contracts and plan the next steps.

We assist in company registration

Lawyers advises on the preparation of documents for the registration process in the relevant registries - KRS or CEIDG. We will prepare documents for registration as a VAT payer - VAT. We will provide support when opening a bank account for the company.

The formal stage of registration is over. From this moment, the company can begin to conduct full-fledged business activities in Poland. We get from Mrs. registrar the necessary documents confirming that the company was registered in the KRS or CEIDG registers.

Legal advice in the
field of obtaining a
residence card

Temporary residence card
(Karta czasowego pobytu)

The residence card is issued for a period of 1 to 3 years. Waiting period: 5 to 7 months. It is possible to issue residence cards on the basis of:
  • official work
  • family reunions
  • studies at the hospital and for absolvents
  • on the basis of doing business
Consultation

Permanent residence card
(Karta stałego pobytu)

The steel residence card is issued for 10 years (resolved ones are issued on a permanent basis). Processing time: 3 to 5 months. It is possible to issue residence cards on the basis of:
  • pole cards
  • Polish origin
  • Polish origin
  • marriage to a Polish citizen
