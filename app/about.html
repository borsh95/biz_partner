About us

BIZ PARTNER is a Polish accounting outsourcing company that specializes in providing comprehensive services in the field of accounting and taxation in Poland

Our team is a team of highly qualified professionals with a strong technical base and able to quickly solve complex problems of clients in the field of accounting and taxation in Poland.

Our goal is to provide business in Poland with the best accounting services, as well as outsourcing of processes related to the interaction of your business with the Polish government.

We also offer additional services to our clients, which include

  • registration of licenses, certificates and other permits
  • registration of all types of residence cards (residence permits) in Poland
  • servicing investments in Poland
  • support of transactions for the purchase of business and commercial real estate

We work with medium and large businesses in Poland

Our clients are famous brands. By entrusting your bookkeeping to us, you can easily deal with other processes without burdening yourself with unnecessary problems.
